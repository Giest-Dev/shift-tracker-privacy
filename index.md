# Privacy Policy

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data entered into the app is stored locally on the user’s device and is never transmitted off the device unless the user explicitly chooses to export it using system sharing features.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or any third-party services.

If you have questions or concerns, you can contact the developer at: elias.hitchcock@outlook.com
